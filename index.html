Chicken Curry

Chicken curry is common to several countries including Bangladesh, countries in Asia and the Caribbean

Ingredients for Chicken Curry

  • Coriander, cumin, turmeic, fennel seeds,
    cinnamon, pepper, ground mustard,
    ground cloves, cayenne pepper and salt.
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh onion, garlic and ginger
  • Low-sodium chicken broth
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Chicken breasts
  • Cornstarch
  • Heavy Cream
  • Chopped cilantro

Steps to make chicken curry

  1. Mix spices together
  2. Saute onion, garlic and giner in a skillet
  3. Add broth and tomatoes, simmer
  4. Blend mixture in a blender as directed then return to skillet
  5. Season sauce then add chicken
  6. Simmer chicken in sauce until cooked through
  7. Add cornstarch slurry during last minute to thicken sauce
  8. Stir in cream
  9. Serve over rice garnished with cilantro
